üìê Governing Principle

A Ponderal Index (PI) Calculator does not adhere to a single global standard like BMI, but rather uses a formula derived from the work of Swiss physician Fritz Rohrer in 1921. The PI is a calculation that relates a person's mass to the third power of their height (mass/height¬≥) and is primarily used in pediatrics for assessing growth and body composition in infants and children. While it is not standardized globally, it is a recognized tool for evaluating body weight relative to length.
