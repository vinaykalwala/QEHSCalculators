How BMI Works

BMI is a measure that uses your height and weight to estimate if you are within a healthy weight range. For adults aged 20 and older, BMI categories are defined as:

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Healthy Weight: 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: 25.0 to 29.9
  • Obesity: 30.0 or higher

Limitations
