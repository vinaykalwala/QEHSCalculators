üß† Understanding This Tool

The HOMA-IR (Homeostatic Model Assessment for Insulin Resistance) is a method used to estimate insulin resistance based on fasting glucose and insulin levels. This tool also provides the QUICKI (Quantitative Insulin Sensitivity Check Index), offering additional insight into how sensitive your body is to insulin.

üìã Parameters & Interpretation

  • HOMA-IR < 1.9: Generally indicates normal insulin sensitivity.
  • HOMA-IR 1.9 - 2.9: May suggest early insulin resistance.
  • HOMA-IR > 2.9: Often indicates significant insulin resistance.
  • QUICKI > 0.339: Usually suggests healthy insulin sensitivity.
  • QUICKI < 0.339: May indicate insulin resistance.

Both values should be from samples taken after at least 8 hours of fasting.

üîç Result Explanation

This calculator provides two key values:

  • HOMA-IR Score: Estimates how resistant your body is to insulin. A higher score means more resistance (less ideal).
  • QUICKI Value: Reflects how sensitive your body is to insulin. A higher value indicates better sensitivity (more ideal).
