What Affects Your BMR?

  • Age: BMR decreases with age due to metabolic changes.
  • Gender: Men usually have higher BMR due to muscle mass.
  • Body composition: More muscle = higher BMR.
  • Height & weight: Larger bodies typically have higher BMR.
