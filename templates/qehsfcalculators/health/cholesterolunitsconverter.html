üìò About Cholesterol Units

Cholesterol and triglyceride levels are measured differently around the world. In the United States and some other countries, the standard unit is **milligrams per deciliter (mg/dL)**. In Canada, the UK, Australia, and much of Europe, the standard is **millimoles per liter (mmol/L)**. This converter allows for easy translation between the two, which is essential for understanding medical literature and personal lab results from different regions.
