How This Calculator Works

This calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)‚Äîthe calories your body burns at rest. It then adjusts this value based on your activity level to find your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE), or maintenance calories. Finally, it subtracts the necessary calories to meet your weekly weight loss goal (approx. 500 calories/day for 0.5 kg/week).

Sustainable Weight Loss

  • Safe Rate: A weight loss of 0.5 to 1 kg per week is generally considered safe and sustainable. Faster weight loss can lead to muscle loss and nutrient deficiencies.
  • Minimum Calories: It is generally not recommended for women to consume fewer than 1,200 calories per day, or for men to consume fewer than 1,500 calories per day, without medical supervision.
