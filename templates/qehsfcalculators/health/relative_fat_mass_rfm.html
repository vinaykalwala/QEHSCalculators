About Relative Fat Mass (RFM)

RFM is a scientifically validated method to estimate body fat percentage using only your height and waist circumference. Unlike BMI, RFM is often considered more accurate for assessing body fatness because it directly accounts for central (abdominal) fat, a key indicator of metabolic health risks.

Measurement Instructions

  • Height: Stand straight against a wall without shoes and measure from the floor to the top of your head.
  • Waist: Keep the tape measure horizontal. For men, measure at the level of the navel. For women, measure at the narrowest part of the torso, usually just above the navel.
  • Take measurements with a relaxed abdomen, without sucking in your stomach.
