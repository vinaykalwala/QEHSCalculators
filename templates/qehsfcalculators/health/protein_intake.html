Why is Protein Essential?

Protein is a vital macronutrient that serves as the building block for tissues, muscles, enzymes, and hormones. Adequate protein intake is crucial for muscle repair and growth, immune function, and maintaining a healthy metabolism. Your specific needs depend on factors like age, weight, and activity level.

General Protein Intake Guidelines (g per kg of body weight)

  • Sedentary Adults: 0.8 g/kg (This is the Recommended Dietary Allowance - RDA).
  • Active Adults: 1.2 - 1.7 g/kg.
  • Building Muscle: 1.6 - 2.2 g/kg.
  • Older Adults (>65): 1.0 - 1.2 g/kg to help prevent age-related muscle loss (sarcopenia).
