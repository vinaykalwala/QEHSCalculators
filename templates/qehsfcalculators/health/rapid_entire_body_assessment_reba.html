About the REBA Method

The Rapid Entire Body Assessment (REBA) is an ergonomic tool used to evaluate the postural risks associated with workplace tasks. It provides a quick and systematic assessment of the musculoskeletal stresses on a worker's entire body, helping to identify tasks that may lead to injury.

Risk Level Interpretation

ScoreRisk LevelAction Required
1-2Low RiskNo action is necessary.
3-4Medium RiskFurther investigation is needed; changes may be required.
5-7High RiskInvestigate and implement changes soon.
8+Very High RiskInvestigate and implement changes immediately.

How to Use This Calculator

This tool calculates the final REBA score from three preliminary scores. A full REBA assessment involves analyzing the worker's posture for the neck, trunk, legs, upper arms, lower arms, and wrists, which are combined into a single "Posture Score". This is then adjusted based on the load/force handled and how good the grip ("coupling") on the object is.
