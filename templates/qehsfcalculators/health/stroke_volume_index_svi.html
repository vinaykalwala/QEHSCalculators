About Stroke Volume Index (SVI)

Stroke Volume Index (SVI) provides a personalized measure of heart efficiency by adjusting the Stroke Volume (SV) for an individual's Body Surface Area (BSA). This normalization allows for a more accurate comparison of cardiac performance between people of different sizes.

Result Interpretation

  • SVI < 33 mL/m¬≤: Considered low and may indicate reduced cardiac output or poor heart function.
  • SVI between 33 and 47 mL/m¬≤: This is the normal range for healthy individuals, indicating efficient cardiac performance.
  • SVI > 47 mL/m¬≤: Considered high, often seen in athletes, reflecting strong and efficient heart function.
