🩺 About Diabetes Risk

This diabetes risk calculator estimates your likelihood of developing type 2 diabetes over the next 7.5 years based on clinical and demographic data.

📊 Understanding the Parameters

This calculator evaluates your risk based on key health indicators:

  • Age: Increasing age is a significant risk factor for developing diabetes.
  • Gender: Biological differences can influence diabetes risk.
  • Height & Weight (BMI): Higher BMI is linked to increased diabetes risk.
  • Family History of Diabetes: Having close relatives with diabetes increases your likelihood of developing the condition.
  • Ethnicity: Certain ethnic groups, such as Latin Americans, have higher risk due to genetic and lifestyle factors.
  • Fasting Glucose: Elevated levels indicate impaired glucose regulation.
  • Systolic Blood Pressure: High blood pressure coexists with diabetes and raises cardiovascular risk.
  • HDL Cholesterol: Low levels of "good" HDL cholesterol are associated with higher diabetes risk.

🛡️ Preventing Diabetes

Even if you have a high risk score, lifestyle changes can significantly reduce your risk:

  • Maintain a healthy, balanced diet rich in fiber and whole grains.
  • Exercise regularly and stay active.
  • Keep a healthy body weight and achieve a healthy BMI.
  • Monitor and control your blood pressure and cholesterol levels.
  • Avoid smoking and manage other health conditions.

✅ Input Validations

  • Age: Enter a value between 18–100 years. Only whole numbers are allowed.
  • Gender: Select Male (0) or Female (1).
  • Height: Enter height in centimeters, between 100–250 cm.
  • Weight: Enter weight in kilograms, between 30–250 kg.
  • Family History: Select No (0) or Yes (1).
  • Ethnicity: Select Non-risk group (0) or Risk group (1).
  • Fasting Glucose: Enter value in mg/dL, between 50–200.
  • Systolic Blood Pressure (BP): Enter value in mmHg, between 80–220.
  • HDL Cholesterol: Enter value in mg/dL, between 20–100.
  • BMI Check: Your height and weight should result in a BMI between 15–50. Values outside this range may be unrealistic.

⚠️ Make sure to fill all fields correctly. Invalid or missing inputs will prevent calculation.
