Water control valve capacity calculation is standardized by the International Society of Automation (ISA) standard ISA-75.01.01, which provides flow equations for sizing control valves. International standards like the European IEC 60534-2-1 offer similar sizing methods, while the standard unit for expressing this capacity is the valve's Flow Coefficient (Cv).

Key Standards
ISA-75.01.01: This is the primary American standard that governs how to calculate the Cv for a control valve, considering fluid properties, flow rate, and pressure drop.
IEC 60534-2-1: This international standard provides a comparable method for sizing control valves with a focus on control performance.
Indian Standard (IS): India often aligns its standards with international norms; for example, IS/IEC 60534-2-1 is the Indian equivalent of the European standard.
