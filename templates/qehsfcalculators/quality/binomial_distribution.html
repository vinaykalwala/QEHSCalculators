📘 About This Calculator

This tool calculates the binomial probability of getting exactly x successes in n trials, based on the probability of success (p). It is useful in statistics, quality checks, and risk analysis

📌 Input Validation

  • Sample Size (n): Required, positive integer (≥1, ≤100,000).
  • Item of Interest (x): Required, integer between 0 and n.
  • Probability (p): Required, decimal between 0 and 1 (max 4 decimals).
  • Logical Consistency: x cannot exceed n; p cannot be negative or >1.
  • Output Range: Probability must be between 0 and 1. Values <0.001 = rare, >0.95 = highly certain.
  • Compliance Note: Validations ensure audit-ready data for OSHA / ISO 45001 / NFPA reporting.

Understanding Parameters:

  • Sample Size (n): The number of trials or experiments.
  • Item of Interest (x): The number of successful outcomes.
  • Probability of Success (p): The probability of success on a single trial.
