🔎 About This Calculator

The Orifice Meter Flow Velocity Calculator estimates fluid velocity through an orifice plate using ΔP, ρ, β, Cₒ, and gc. It is simple, cost-effective, and standardized under ISO 5167 and ASME MFC-3M, widely used in process control and compliance.

❓ Key Parameters

  • ΔP (Pa): Pressure drop, must be > 0.
  • ρ (kg/m³): Fluid density, > 0.
  • β: Diameter ratio (d/D), valid 0.2–0.8.
  • Cₒ: Discharge coefficient, 0.6–0.65.
  • gc: Conversion factor, usually 1 (SI).
  • V̄ₒ (m/s): Calculated flow velocity.

🔎 Input Validations

  • ΔP (Pa): >0 and ≤1,000,000 Pa
  • ρ (kg/m³): >0, typical 0.1–2000
  • β: 0.2–0.8 only
  • Cₒ: 0.6–0.65

📊 Output Interpretation

  • <1 m/s → ⚠️ Too low, unreliable
  • 1–50 m/s → ✅ Acceptable
  • >50 m/s → ⚠️ Too high, check inputs
