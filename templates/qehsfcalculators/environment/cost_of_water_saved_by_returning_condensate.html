Assessment & Suggestions

📘 About

This calculator estimates annual water cost savings from improved condensate return in boiler systems. All values should use consistent units.

📏 Units & typical ranges

  • Improvement in Condensate Return (X): Percentage (%). Typical range: 5-30%.
  • Boiler Evaporation Rate (C): kg/h. Varies by boiler size and capacity.
  • Operational Hours (D): h/year. Typically 6,000-8,000 h/year for continuous operations.
  • Cost of Water (W): ₹/m³. Varies by location and water source.

⚠️ Strong validations (what is checked)

  • All inputs are required and must be finite numbers.
  • X ≥ 0 - Improvement percentage must be non-negative.
  • C > 0 - Evaporation rate must be positive.
  • D > 0 - Operational hours must be positive.
  • W > 0 - Water cost must be positive.
  • X ≤ 100 - Improvement percentage cannot exceed 100%.
  • D ≤ 8760 - Operational hours cannot exceed hours in a year.
  • Extreme values will trigger warnings for potential unit errors.

🔎 Interpretation

  • High savings (> ₹500,000/year): Significant water cost reduction - excellent return on investment.
  • Moderate savings (₹50,000-500,000/year): Substantial water savings - good investment opportunity.
  • Low savings (< ₹50,000/year): Modest water cost reduction - evaluate based on implementation costs.
  • Zero savings: No improvement in condensate return.

🛑 Limitations

  • Assumes linear relationship between condensate return improvement and water savings.
  • Does not account for implementation costs of condensate return systems.
  • Does not consider water treatment costs for make-up water.
  • Assumes constant water prices over time.
  • Does not account for maintenance costs of condensate return systems.
