📘 About This Calculator

The Carbon Footprint Calculator determines the environmental impact of travel activities by calculating CO₂ emissions. This helps individuals and organizations understand their contribution to climate change and make informed decisions about sustainable transportation options.

📊 Interpretation

  • Footprint < 50 kg CO₂ → ✅ Low environmental impact (Excellent)
  • 50 ≤ Footprint ≤ 200 kg CO₂ → ⚠️ Moderate environmental impact (Consider alternatives)
  • Footprint > 200 kg CO₂ → 🔴 High environmental impact (Significant impact)

⚠️ Input Validations

  • ✔️ Distance Traveled must be a numeric, positive value
  • ✔️ Minimum Distance: 0.1 miles (meaningful measurement)
  • ⚠️ Distance > 10,000 miles → warning (extreme distance, verify data)
  • ✔️ Emission Factor must be a numeric, positive value
  • ✔️ Minimum Emission Factor: 0.01 kg CO₂/mile
  • ⚠️ Emission Factor > 5.0 kg CO₂/mile → warning (extremely high, verify source)
  • ❌ Zero, negative, or non-numeric inputs are invalid
  • ⚠️ Extreme emissions > 10,000 kg CO₂ → warning (unrealistic, verify data)

📏 Typical Emission Factors

  • Gasoline Car: 0.411 kg CO₂/mile
  • Diesel Car: 0.481 kg CO₂/mile
  • Electric Car: 0.2 kg CO₂/mile (varies by electricity source)
  • Bus: 0.13 kg CO₂/mile (per passenger)
  • Train: 0.1 kg CO₂/mile (varies by type)
  • Commercial Airplane: 0.2 kg CO₂/passenger mile
  • Bicycle/Walking: 0.0 kg CO₂/mile

🌍 Key Applications

  • Corporate Sustainability: Tracking and reducing business travel emissions
  • Environmental Reporting: Compliance with carbon disclosure requirements
  • Personal Carbon Accounting: Understanding individual environmental impact
  • Transportation Planning: Evaluating eco-friendly transportation options
  • Carbon Offset Programs: Calculating emissions for offset purchases

🛑 Limitations

  • Does not account for vehicle manufacturing emissions
  • Assumes average emission factors (varies by specific vehicle and conditions)
  • Does not include indirect emissions from infrastructure
  • Simplified calculation - real-world emissions can vary significantly
  • Does not consider occupancy rates for shared transportation
