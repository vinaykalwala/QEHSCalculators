📘 About This Calculator

The Population Percent Growth Calculator estimates the population at a future time based on a constant percent growth rate, supporting planning and resource allocation.

📊 Interpretation

  • Growth Rate > 0 → ✅ Population is increasing
  • Growth Rate = 0 → ℹ️ Population remains constant
  • Growth Rate < 0 → ⚠️ Population is decreasing

⚠️ Validations

  • ✔️ Initial Population must be ≥ 0
  • ✔️ Growth Rate can be positive, negative, or zero (expressed as decimal)
  • ✔️ Number of Periods must be ≥ 0
  • ❌ Non-numeric or empty entries are invalid
  • ⚠️ Extremely large projected populations (>1e10) will trigger warnings
  • ⚠️ High growth rate with long periods can produce unrealistic projections — verify inputs
  • ⚠️ More than 6 decimal places in any input will trigger a warning

🌍 Key Applications

  • Urban Planning: Predicting future population for infrastructure development
  • Resource Management: Estimating future demand for water, energy, and housing
  • Demographics: Analyzing population trends for policy-making
  • Finance: Modeling compound interest growth
  • Ecology: Modeling population growth or decline in ecosystems
