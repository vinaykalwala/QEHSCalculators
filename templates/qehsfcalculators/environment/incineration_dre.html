📘 About This Calculator

This calculator estimates the Destruction & Removal Efficiency (DRE) for a target compound in an incinerator or thermal oxidizer, using its measured mass feed rate and stack emission rate. Use the same compound, same averaging period, and same units for both values. Results support compliance checks and performance optimization.

📊 Interpretation

  • DRE < 90% → 🔴 Low efficiency (fails most standards)
  • 90% ≤ DRE < 99% → ⚠️ Moderate (improvement needed)
  • 99% ≤ DRE < 99.99% → ⚠️ High (often acceptable but check permit)
  • DRE ≥ 99.99% → ✅ Excellent (meets typical hazardous waste criteria)

Thresholds are common industry targets; always verify against your site permit/regulatory conditions.

⚠️ Input Validations

  • Units: choose kg/h or lb/h (applied to both inputs).
  • Win > 0 (required, numeric). Recommended reporting ≤ 6 decimals.
  • Wout ≥ 0 (required, numeric). Recommended reporting ≤ 6 decimals.
  • Logical consistency: Wout ≤ Win.
  • Sanity warnings (advisory): very small Win (< 1e-6) or very large Win (> 1e5) for a single compound.
  • Reject non-finite results or DRE outside [0, 100].
  • If Wout is zero or near zero, DRE will approach 100% (consider detection limits).

🌍 Key Applications

  • Hazardous waste incineration: Trial burns, compliance checks, and performance tuning.
  • Medical/industrial waste: Verify destruction of VOCs/POHCs.
  • Air permits: Demonstrate removal efficiency vs. regulatory thresholds.
