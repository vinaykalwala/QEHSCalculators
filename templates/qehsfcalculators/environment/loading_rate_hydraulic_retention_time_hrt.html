📘 About This Calculator

The Hydraulic Retention Time (HRT) measures how long wastewater or liquid stays in a tank. Proper HRT ensures effective treatment, pollutant removal, and compliance with environmental regulations.

⚠️ Input Validations

  • ✔️ Tank Volume (V): Must be positive, realistic range 0.1–1,000,000 m³ depending on system size.
  • ✔️ Influent Flow Rate (Q): Must be positive, realistic range 0.01–500,000 m³/day.
  • ❌ Non-numeric, zero, or negative inputs are invalid.
  • ⚠️ HRT less than 0.1 days may indicate insufficient treatment time.
  • ⚠️ HRT exceeding 100 days may be impractical for most industrial or municipal systems.

🌍 Key Applications

  • Wastewater Treatment: Ensures adequate retention for biological and chemical processes.
  • Industrial Water Treatment: Designs retention tanks for effluent processing.
  • Aquaculture: Maintains water quality in fish farming tanks.
  • Environmental Compliance: Monitors treatment efficiency and regulatory adherence.
