📘 About This Calculator

The Metal Loss Calculator determines the thickness reduction of a metal component due to corrosion or wear, which is vital for equipment safety and maintenance planning.

⚠️Input Validations

  • ✔️ Previous Thickness must be a positive value (≥ 0.001 inches).
  • ✔️ Current Thickness must be a positive value (≥ 0.001 inches).
  • ❌ Current Thickness cannot be greater than Previous Thickness.
  • ⚠️ If Metal Loss = 0 → No degradation detected (check inputs).
  • ⚠️ If Current Thickness < 10% of Previous Thickness → Severe degradation warning.
  • ⚠️ Thickness values above 20 inches may be unrealistic for most industrial applications.

🌍 Key Applications

  • Pipelines: Measuring corrosion in oil, gas, and water transport.
  • Storage Tanks: Assessing wall thinning in chemical and fuel tanks.
  • Industrial Equipment: Monitoring wear in boilers, heat exchangers, and machinery.
  • Marine Structures: Evaluating hull and offshore platform corrosion.
