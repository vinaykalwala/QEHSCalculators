📘 About This Calculator

The Air Flow Rate Calculator determines the volumetric flow of air through a given cross-sectional area. This is essential for designing and monitoring ventilation systems.

⚙️ Parameters Used

  • Velocity (m/s): Speed of air moving through the duct or space.
  • Cross-Sectional Area (m²): Area through which air flows.

📊 How to Use

  • Enter the Velocity of the air in m/s.
  • Enter the Cross-Sectional Area in m².
  • Click Calculate Flow Rate to see the airflow in m³/s.

🌬️ Mass Flow Rate Information

Mass Flow Rate represents the mass of air passing through a section per unit time. It is derived from the volumetric airflow and is essential for proper ventilation system design, duct sizing, and HVAC performance analysis.

  • Air Density: The mass per unit volume of air, usually considered at standard conditions.
  • Relation to Flow Rate: The mass flow rate depends on the volumetric airflow calculated by this tool.
