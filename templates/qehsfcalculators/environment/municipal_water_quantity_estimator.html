📘 About

This estimator computes total daily water demand by multiplying the per-capita water demand (liters per person per day) by the population served. Use consistent SI units: PCD in liters/day/person, population in persons.

⚠️ Input validations

  • Per-capita demand (PCD) — required, numeric, > 0. Typical urban ranges: 20–500 L/day/person. Hard-limit error if ≤ 0.1 or > 10,000 (likely units error).
  • Population — required, numeric, ≥ 1. Should be an integer (fractions will be rounded with a warning). Hard-limit error if > 1,000,000,000.
  • Precision — avoid excessive decimals; inputs with > 3 decimal places will produce a precision warning.
  • Cross-checks — Extremely large totals (≫ 1×10⁶ L/day) will trigger warnings; totals > 1×10⁹ L/day produce an error asking you to check units and inputs.

🏭 Applications

  • Urban water supply & distribution planning
  • Booster pump and storage sizing
  • Fire flow / emergency water availability checks
  • Environmental and wastewater load estimations
