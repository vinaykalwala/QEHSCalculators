📘 About This Calculator

This tool evaluates the balance of Bernoulli’s equation

📊 Interpretation

  • ✅ LHS ≈ RHS (within tolerance): Bernoulli holds for given flow.
  • ⚠️ Small imbalance: Likely rounding/measurement errors.
  • 🔴 Large imbalance: Inputs may be incorrect or conditions violate Bernoulli assumptions.

⚠️ Input Validations

  • ✅ Pressure (P₁, P₂): Must be a valid number in Pa. Can be positive or negative but within reasonable limits (e.g., ±10,000,000 Pa).
  • ✅ Velocity (U₁, U₂): Must be ≥ 0 m/s. Extremely high velocities (>200 m/s) may trigger warnings.
  • ✅ Fluid Density (ρ): Must be positive (kg/m³). Typical range: 50–20,000 kg/m³. Values outside this range will trigger a warning.
  • ⚠️ Large Velocity Differences: If |U₁ - U₂| > 100 m/s, a warning is displayed.
  • ⚠️ Extreme Pressures: Pressures beyond ±10,000,000 Pa trigger a warning.
  • ⚠️ Unusual Density: ρ < 50 or > 20,000 kg/m³ triggers a warning.
  • ❌ Invalid or empty fields will stop calculation and show errors above results.

🌍 Key Applications

  • Hydraulics: Flow through pipes and ducts.
  • Aerodynamics: Airflow around wings and structures.
  • Process Engineering: Pressure–velocity relationships.
