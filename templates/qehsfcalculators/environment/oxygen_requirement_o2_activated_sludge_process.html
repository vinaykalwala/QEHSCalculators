📘 About

This calculator determines the Oxygen Requirement (O₂) for the Activated Sludge Process in wastewater treatment systems using the formula:

O₂ = [Q(S₀ − S) − 1.42 Qw Xr] / f

✅ Input Validations

  • All flow rates (Q, Qw) and concentrations (S0, S, Xr) must be positive numbers.
  • Effluent BOD (S) must be less than Influent BOD (S0).
  • BOD fraction (f) must be between 0.6 and 0.85.
  • Check that Q(S0 − S) > 1.42 × Qw × Xr to avoid negative oxygen requirement.
  • Units must be consistent: m³/day for flow, mg/L for concentrations, dimensionless for f.

🏭 Applications

  • Municipal sewage treatment – aeration tank design.
  • Industrial effluent treatment – process optimization.
  • Environmental compliance – calculating oxygen demand for audits.
