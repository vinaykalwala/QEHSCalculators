📘 About

This calculator computes the Mass of Waste Activated Sludge in biological wastewater treatment systems. Inputs must use consistent units: flows in m³/day, concentrations in mg/L, reactor volume in m³, and rates in 1/day.

✅ Input Validations

  • Y: 0 < Y ≤ 1. Typical microbial yield coefficient.
  • Q (m³/day): 0.001 – 10,000,000
  • So, Se (mg/L): 0 ≤ Se < So ≤ 5000. Effluent must be lower than influent.
  • kd (1/day): 0 ≤ kd ≤ 1
  • X (mg/L): 50 – 20,000
  • V (m³): 0.01 – 1,000,000
  • Decimals: max 3 for concentrations and flows; max 4 for reactor volume.
  • Logical consistency: Se must be less than So; all inputs positive; computed mass must be ≥0.

🏭 Applications

  • Wastewater Treatment Plants: Plan sludge handling, optimize aeration and wasting rates.
  • Environmental Compliance: Ensure proper solids disposal and discharge standards.
  • Process Control: Adjust operational parameters to maintain microbial growth and minimize excess sludge.
  • Sludge Management: Predict mass of waste sludge for energy/cost optimization.
