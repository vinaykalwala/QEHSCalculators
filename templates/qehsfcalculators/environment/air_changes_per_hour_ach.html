📘 About This Calculator

The ACH Calculator determines the number of times per hour that the total volume of air in a space is replaced, based on the airflow rate and the space’s volume.

📊 Interpretation

  • ACH ≥ 6 → 🌟 Excellent ventilation.
  • ACH 4–6 → ✅ Good ventilation.
  • ACH 2–4 → 🟢 Fair ventilation.
  • ACH < 2 → 🔴 Poor ventilation.

⚠️ Limitations

While ACH is a useful measure of ventilation effectiveness, it does not directly account for airflow distribution, filtration efficiency, or pollutant sources inside the space. Always combine ACH values with other indoor air quality assessments for accurate evaluation.

✔️ Input Validations

  • Air Flow Rate (Q): Must be > 0 and ≤ 100,000 m³/h (practical HVAC/ventilation range).
  • Room Volume (V): Must be > 0 and ≤ 50,000 m³ (typical for residential, commercial, or industrial spaces).
  • Decimals: Up to 2 decimal places allowed for precision.
  • Logical Check: If Room Volume = 0 → ACH cannot be calculated (division by zero).
  • Practical Check: ACH values usually range from 2–20 for normal spaces; >20 indicates special environments like hospitals or labs.

🌍 Key Applications

  • Hospitals: Infection control through air replacement.
  • Laboratories & Cleanrooms: Ensuring contamination-free environments.
  • Commercial & Residential Spaces: Maintaining healthy indoor air quality.
