📘 About This Calculator

This calculator determines the fluid velocity in a pipe. Fluid velocity is a critical KPI for pipeline integrity, pump sizing, and QEHS compliance.

📊Input Validations

  • ✔️ Flow Rate (Q) must be ≥ 0.0001 m³/s (zero flow is invalid).
  • ✔️ Pipe Diameter (D) must be ≥ 0.01 m.
  • ✔️ Both inputs must be numeric.
  • ⚠️ Extremely high flow rates (>10 m³/s) may indicate unrealistic inputs.
  • ⚠️ Extremely small diameters (<0.01 m) may produce dangerously high velocities.
  • ⚠️ Low velocities (<0.6 m/s) risk sedimentation and reduced efficiency.
  • ⚠️ High velocities (>3 m/s) risk pipe erosion and safety hazards.

🌍 Applications & Safety Relevance

  • Water Supply Systems: Prevent sedimentation and pipe wear.
  • HVAC Piping: Ensure air/water velocity avoids noise or vibration.
  • Chemical/Industrial Piping: Avoid unsafe erosion and maintain system integrity.
  • Fire Safety Systems: Ensure sufficient velocity for sprinkler operation.
