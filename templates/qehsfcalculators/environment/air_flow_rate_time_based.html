📘 About This Calculator

The Air Flow Rate (Time-Based) Calculator determines airflow by dividing the measured air volume by the time taken. Useful in ventilation, HVAC, and safety engineering.

❓ What is Air Flow Rate?

Air Flow Rate is the volume of air passing through a system per unit of time, usually expressed in m³/s.

⚙️ Parameters Used

  • Air Volume (m³): The total volume of air moved.
  • Time (s): The duration over which the air moves.

📊 Interpretation

  • Higher flow rate → Better ventilation / cooling efficiency
  • Moderate flow rate → Acceptable, but can be improved
  • Low flow rate → Poor air circulation, needs attention
