📘 About This Calculator

The Weir Flow Rate Calculator determines the flow rate over a rectangular weir, which is essential for water resource management, environmental monitoring, and hydraulic engineering applications.

📐 Parameters

  • Width of Weir (b): The horizontal length of the weir crest in meters.
  • Head over Weir (h): The vertical height of water above the weir crest in meters.

✅ Input Validations

✔️ Width (b) must be positive and greater than 0.01 m.
✔️ Head (h) must be positive and greater than 0.01 m.
❌ Negative or zero values are invalid.
⚠️ Head values below 0.03 m may be inaccurate due to surface tension.
⚠️ Head values above 1.5 m may decrease accuracy.

📊 Output Validations

  • Q > 2 m³/s → ✅ High Flow: Suitable for large-scale applications.
  • 0.5 ≤ Q ≤ 2 m³/s → ⚠️ Moderate Flow: Appropriate for medium-scale or municipal systems.
  • Q < 0.5 m³/s → 🔴 Low Flow: May not be efficient for standard operations.

📋 Standards & Compliance

This calculator follows standard hydraulic engineering practices and aligns with:

  • ISO 4360:2008 - Hydrometry - Open channel flow measurement using triangular profile weirs
  • ASTM D1941 - Standard Test Method for Open Channel Flow Measurement of Water with the Parshall Flume
  • Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) guidelines for water monitoring
