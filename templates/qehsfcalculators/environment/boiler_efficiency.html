📘 About This Calculator

The Boiler Efficiency Calculator determines how effectively a boiler converts fuel energy into useful steam energy, reducing energy waste and operating costs.

📊 Interpretation

  • Efficiency < 60% → 🔴 Poor efficiency (High energy loss).
  • 60% ≤ Efficiency ≤ 85% → ⚠️ Moderate efficiency (Needs optimization).
  • Efficiency > 85% → ✅ High efficiency (Good fuel utilization).

⚠️ Validations

✔️ Heat Exported in Steam must be a positive value.
✔️ Heat Provided by Fuel must be a positive value.
❌ Zero or negative values are invalid.
❌ Non-numeric entries are invalid.

🌍 Key Applications

  • Power Plants: Efficiency monitoring and optimization.
  • Industrial Boilers: Energy cost reduction & performance tracking.
  • Energy Audits: Compliance with energy-saving standards.
