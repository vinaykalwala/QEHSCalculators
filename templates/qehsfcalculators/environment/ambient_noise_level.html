📘 About This Calculator

Determines the permissible ambient noise level for different zones and times, helping maintain compliance with CPCB/ISO 11690/Occupational Safety standards.

⚠️ Input Validations

  • ✔️ Zone must be selected (Industrial, Residential, Commercial, Silence).
  • ✔️ Time of Day must be selected (Day/Night).
  • ✔️ Silence Zone is most sensitive; noise limits are lower (40-50 dB).
  • ⚠️ Industrial areas should not exceed 75 dB (day) or 70 dB (night).

🌍 Key Applications

  • Industrial workplaces – monitoring machine and process noise for OSHA compliance.
  • Residential and commercial areas – ensuring ambient noise is within CPCB limits.
  • Silence zones – hospitals, schools, and sensitive areas.
  • Audits and inspections – verifying compliance with ISO 11690 and local noise regulations.
  • Occupational health – protecting workers from long-term hearing damage.
  • Urban planning – ensuring community noise standards are met during day/night.

📊 Interpretation

  • 📗 ≤ Limit → Acceptable noise level, safe for people.
  • 📙 5–10 dB above limit → Moderate noise, may cause discomfort.
  • 📕 > 10 dB above limit → High noise, requires mitigation measures.
