📘 About This Calculator

This calculator estimates the effluent BOD concentration from a Completely Mixed Flow Reactor (CMFR) based on the influent BOD, reaction rate constant, and detention time. It also calculates the overall treatment efficiency by showing the percentage of BOD removed.

📊 Interpretation (guideline)

  • ✅ High removal: Efficiency > 85% — very good performance.
  • ⚠️ Moderate removal: 50% ≤ Efficiency ≤ 85% — may be acceptable; check operating conditions.
  • 🔴 Low removal: Efficiency < 50% — investigate process, increase detention or treatment intensity.

⚠️ Input Validations

  • ✔️ Li (Influent BOD) must be numeric and > 0 mg/L. Typical domestic range: 50–400 mg/L. Values < 10 mg/L may indicate already-treated water; values > 2000 mg/L should be double-checked (industrial waste).
  • ✔️ k1 must be numeric and > 0 (1/day). Typical for domestic wastewater at ambient temp: 0.05–0.30 1/day. Values outside 0.01–1.0 1/day will trigger a warning to verify.
  • ✔️ t must be numeric and > 0 (days). Typical reactor detention: 0.1–10 days. Very short (< 0.1 day) or extremely long (> 30 days) values will trigger warnings.
  • ❌ Non-numeric, zero, or negative inputs are invalid and will be rejected.
  • ⚠️ If computed Le ≥ Li (unexpected), check inputs — may indicate wrong signs or invalid k1/t.

🌍 Key Applications

  • Municipal aeration basins, stabilization ponds
  • Design and optimization of biological reactors
  • Performance evaluation and compliance checks
