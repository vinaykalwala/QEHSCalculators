📘 About

This calculator estimates steam required to heat a process fluid by ΔT. It assumes steady-state conditions and ignores heat losses. Inputs must be in consistent SI units.

⚠️ Input Validations

  • ṁ (kg/s): > 0. Typical: 0.0001 – 10000.
  • cₚ (kJ/kg·°C): > 0. Typical: 0.1 – 10.
  • ΔT (°C): > 0. Typical: 0.1 – 200.
  • hfg (kJ/kg): > 0. Typical: 100 – 3000.
  • Decimal precision: warning if > 4 decimals.

🏭 Typical Applications

  • Process heating: Heat exchangers, reboilers, jacketed vessels.
  • HVAC & comfort heating: Air handling units, humidifiers, radiators.
  • Food & beverage: Pasteurizers, sterilizers, cooking kettles.
  • Chemical & pharma: Batch reactors, distillation, drying systems.
  • Utilities & power: Boiler feedwater preheating, deaerators, condensate recovery.
  • Fire & safety systems: Steam smothering, inerting, and emergency heating duties.
