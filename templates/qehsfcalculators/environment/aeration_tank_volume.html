📘 About This Calculator

The Aeration Tank Volume Calculator estimates the required tank size for biological wastewater treatment using the activated sludge process.

📊 Interpretation

  • V ≥ 1000 m³ → ⚠️ Large Tank Volume: Optimization may be needed.
  • 10 m³ ≤ V < 1000 m³ → ✅ Practical volume for operation.
  • V < 10 m³ → 🔴 Very small, may be impractical.

Input Validations

✔️ Flow Rate (Q) must be > 0 and ≤ 100,000 m³/day.
✔️ Mixed Liquor Suspended Solids (MLSS) must be within 1000–5000 mg/L (typical design range).
✔️ Food-to-Microorganism Ratio (F/M) must be between 0.1–1.0 (kg BOD/kg MLSS·day).
✔️ Influent BOD (S₀) must be ≥ Effluent BOD (S).
❌ Negative, zero, or extreme outlier values are not acceptable.
❌ If MLSS = 0 → Tank volume cannot be calculated (division by zero).

🌍 Key Applications

  • Wastewater Treatment Plants: Aeration tank sizing.
  • Industrial Effluent Treatment: Designing bioreactors.
  • Environmental Engineering: Process optimization.
