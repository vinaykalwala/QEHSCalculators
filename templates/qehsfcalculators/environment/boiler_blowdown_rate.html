📘 About This Calculator

The Boiler Blowdown Rate Calculator determines the blowdown required to control Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) in boiler water, ensuring efficiency and preventing scaling/corrosion.

📊 Interpretation

  • BDR < 5 kg/h → ✅ Low blowdown rate (Efficient operation).
  • 5 ≤ BDR ≤ 15 kg/h → ⚠️ Moderate blowdown rate (Monitor boiler condition).
  • BDR > 15 kg/h → 🔴 High blowdown rate (Water wastage / poor TDS control).

📐 Parameters

  • F (Feedwater TDS): Total Dissolved Solids in feedwater (ppm).
  • S (Steam Generation Rate): Amount of steam generated by boiler (kg/h).
  • B (Boiler Water TDS): Total Dissolved Solids in boiler water (ppm).

⚠️ Validations

✔️ Feedwater TDS (F) must be positive.
✔️ Steam Generation Rate (S) must be positive.
✔️ Boiler Water TDS (B) must be greater than Feedwater TDS.
❌ Negative or zero values are invalid.

🌍 Key Applications

  • Power Plants: Boiler efficiency maintenance.
  • Industrial Boilers: Scaling & corrosion control.
  • Water Conservation: Optimize blowdown cycles to reduce wastage.
