🔵 About This Calculator

The Energy Consumption Calculator estimates a device’s daily energy use for audits, energy management, and safety reviews.

📊 Interpretation (context-dependent)

  • 🟢 Energy < 1 kWh/day → Low use (small appliances/IT gear)
  • 🟠 1 ≤ Energy ≤ 5 kWh/day → Moderate (monitor duty cycle/efficiency)
  • 🔴 Energy > 5 kWh/day → High (review scheduling & capacity)

⚠️Input Validations

  • ✔️ Device Wattage must be ≥ 0.1 W
  • ✔️ Hours per Day must be 0–24
  • ✔️ At most 2 decimal places
  • ❌ Non-numeric, negative, or empty entries are invalid
  • ⚠️ W > 2000 → high load; check circuit
  • ⚠️ Hours ≥ 20 → nearly continuous operation
  • ⚠️ Energy > 50 kWh/day → very high; verify
  • ⚠️ Energy > 100 kWh/day → extreme; check measurement

🌍 Key Applications

  • Energy Audits: Identify high-use equipment
  • QEHS Reviews: Check thermal load & safety
  • Operations: Optimize schedules & cycles
  • Reporting: KPI roll-ups for monthly bills
