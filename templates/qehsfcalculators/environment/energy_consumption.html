📘 About This Calculator

The Energy Consumption Calculator estimates a device’s daily energy use for audits, energy management, and safety reviews.

📊 Interpretation (context-dependent)

  • Energy < 1 kWh/day → ✅ Low use (typical small appliances/IT gear)
  • 1 ≤ Energy ≤ 5 kWh/day → ⚠️ Moderate (monitor duty cycle/efficiency)
  • Energy > 5 kWh/day → 🔴 High (review scheduling, efficiency, and circuit capacity)

⚠️ Validations

  • ✔️ Device Wattage must be a positive number (≥ 0.1 W)
  • ✔️ Hours Used per Day must be between 0 and 24
  • ✔️ At most 2 decimal places for both inputs
  • ❌ Non-numeric, negative, or empty entries are invalid
  • ⚠️ W > 2000 → high load; check circuit rating and heat
  • ⚠️ Hours ≥ 20 or = 24 → near/fully continuous operation; check duty cycle
  • ⚠️ Energy > 50 kWh/day → very high; verify need/scheduling
  • ⚠️ Energy > 100 kWh/day → extreme; verify measurement and electrical capacity

🌍 Key Applications

  • Energy Audits: Identify high-use equipment
  • QEHS Reviews: Check thermal loading & circuit capacity
  • Operations: Optimize schedules & duty cycles
  • Reporting: Daily KPI roll-ups for monthly/annual bills
