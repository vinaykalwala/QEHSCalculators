Calculation Details

📘 About

This calculator estimates the maximum ground-level concentration of a pollutant per unit emission rate. It is typically used for air dispersion assessments, emission control planning, and emergency safety audits.

✅ Input Validations

  • Effective Stack Height (H): 0.5–2000 m; must be numeric and positive.
  • Coefficients a: -200 to 200; numeric.
  • Coefficient b: -100 to 100; numeric.
  • Coefficient c: -12 to 12; numeric.
  • Coefficient d: -2 to 2; numeric.
  • Stability Class: One of A, B, C, D, E, F; required.
  • Decimals: H ≤3 dp, coefficients ≤6 dp; warnings issued if exceeded.
  • Exponent safety: a + b ln(H) + c (ln(H))² + d (ln(H))³ must be ≤700 to avoid overflow.

🏭 Applications

  • Air quality compliance assessments.
  • Stack emission control planning.
  • Emergency response and evacuation planning.
  • Fire & safety dispersion simulations.
  • Industrial pollutant risk analysis.

🛑 Limitations

  • Empirical formula; accuracy depends on correct coefficients.
  • Does not account for complex terrain or chemical reactions.
  • Not suitable for multi-stack interference scenarios without adjustments.
  • Results sensitive to H and coefficient magnitudes; extreme values may produce unrealistic outputs.
