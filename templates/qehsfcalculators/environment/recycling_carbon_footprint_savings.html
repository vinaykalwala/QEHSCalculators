📘 About This Calculator

The Recycling CO₂ Savings Calculator estimates the carbon footprint reduction when recycling different materials, based on standard CO₂ saving factors per unit of material.

⚠️Input Validations

  • ✔️ Material must be selected
  • ✔️ Weight must be numeric and ≥ 0.01
  • ✔️ Unit must be selected (kg or lbs)
  • ❌ Negative or zero weight is invalid
  • ❌ Non-numeric entries are invalid
  • ⚠️ Extremely high weight (> 10,000 kg or 22,000 lbs) will trigger warnings

🌍 Key Applications

  • Household Recycling: Track personal impact
  • Industrial Recycling: Assess corporate sustainability efforts
  • Environmental Reporting: Include CO₂ savings in ESG reports
