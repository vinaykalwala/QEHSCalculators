About the ASME Section VIII Formula

The formula provided calculates the minimum required vent area for critical (choked) flow of gas or vapor from a pressure vessel, as specified by ASME Section VIII. This is crucial for ensuring that a pressure relief device can discharge fluid at a sufficient rate to prevent catastrophic overpressure events.
