📘 About This Calculator

The Percentage Risk Index Calculator measures the proportional contribution of a specific risk to the total risk profile. It supports effective risk prioritization, resource allocation, and compliance with safety standards.

⚙️ Parameters Used

  • Relative Risk: Risk score/value for the specific hazard or item.
  • Total Risk: Aggregate risk score of all hazards/items combined.

✅ Input Validation Rules

  • Relative Risk: 0 – 1,000,000 (non-negative, decimals allowed).
  • Total Risk: 0.0001 – 1,000,000,000 (must be greater than 0, decimals allowed).
  • Cross-check: Relative Risk ≤ Total Risk.
  • Edge Cases: Division by zero prevented, empty inputs flagged, unrealistic outliers blocked.

📊 Output Validations

  • Always returns a percentage (2 decimal places).
  • Assessment feedback messages appear with results:
    • 0% → ✅ Zero risk contribution – no action needed.
    • < 5% → ✅ Low priority risk – monitoring sufficient.
    • 5% – 20% → 🔵 Moderate risk – consider mitigation.
    • 20% – 50% → ⚠️ Significant risk – requires attention.
    • > 50% → 🚨 Critical risk – immediate mitigation needed.

📋 Standards

This calculator aligns with recognized frameworks: ISO 31000 (Risk Management), ISO 31010 (Risk Assessment Techniques), OSHA (Occupational Safety & Health), NFPA 551 (Hazard Evaluation), and ANSI/ASSP Z590.3 (Prevention through Design).
