📘 About This Calculator

The Accident Rate Calculator helps evaluate workplace safety by measuring the number of recordable incidents per 1,000,000 hours worked.

❓ What is Accident Rate?

Accident Rate is a standardized safety metric that shows how frequently employees are injured or involved in incidents during work hours.

⚙️ Parameters Used

  • Recordable Incidents: Work-related injuries, illnesses, and fatalities.
  • Total Hours Worked: The cumulative employee hours during the period.

🧮 Formula

Accident Rate = (Number of Incidents ÷ Total Hours Worked) × 1,000,000

📊 Interpretation

  • Below 1.0 → Excellent safety
  • 1.0 – 3.0 → Acceptable but can improve
  • Above 3.0 → Needs serious safety review
