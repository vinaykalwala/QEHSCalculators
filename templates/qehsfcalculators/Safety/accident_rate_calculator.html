About This Calculator

The Accident Rate Calculator measures workplace safety performance by calculating the number of recordable incidents per 1,000,000 hours worked.

Formula: Accident Rate = (Number of Incidents ÷ Total Hours Worked) × 1,000,000

  • Recordable Incidents: Work-related injuries, illnesses, and fatalities per OSHA criteria.
  • Total Hours Worked: Sum of all employee hours during the measured period.
  • Lower rates indicate better safety performance; rates below 1.0 are typically considered good.
