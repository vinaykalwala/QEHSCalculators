This calculator determines the required **relief area for subcritical flow** of gas or vapor. Subcritical flow occurs when downstream pressure ($P_2$) is high enough (typically >55% of upstream pressure) to influence the flow rate through the relief device. Proper sizing ensures safe pressure relief during non-emergency scenarios while maintaining stable flow conditions.
