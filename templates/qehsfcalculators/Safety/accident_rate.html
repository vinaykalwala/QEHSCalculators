📘 About This Calculator

The Accident Rate Calculator helps determine the frequency of workplace accidents per 1,000,000 hours worked, providing a standardized metric to assess safety performance.

❓ What is Accident Rate?

The Accident Rate is a key safety metric that measures how many accidents occur per 1,000,000 hours worked, allowing for comparison across different organizations and industries.

⚙️ Parameters Used

  • Number of Accidents: This includes reportable workplace accidents such as injuries, fatalities, and lost-time incidents.
  • Total Hours Worked: The total number of hours worked by all employees over a specific period.

📊 Interpretation

  • 0.0 → Excellent safety performance with no accidents
  • Below 1.0 → Good safety performance with minimal accidents
  • 1.0 – 5.0 → Moderate safety performance needing improvement
  • Above 5.0 → Poor safety performance requiring immediate action

💡 Important Note

A lower accident rate indicates better safety performance. Regular monitoring of this metric helps organizations identify safety issues and implement preventive measures to protect workers.
