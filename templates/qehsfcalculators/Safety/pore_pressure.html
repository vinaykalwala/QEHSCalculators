üìè Governing Principle

Pore pressure calculations involve a combination of theoretical principles, empirical correlations, and industry standards depending on the application, whether geotechnical or oil and gas.

These principles ensure accurate pore pressure prediction for safe drilling, wellbore stability, and compliance with geotechnical and oil & gas safety standards.
