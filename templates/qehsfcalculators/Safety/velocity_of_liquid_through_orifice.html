About Torricelli's Law

This calculator uses Torricelli‚Äôs Law, a principle in fluid dynamics that relates the speed of fluid flowing out of an orifice to the height of the fluid above the opening. The law states that the exit velocity ($v$) of a fluid from a hole in an open container is the same as the speed a body would attain in free-falling from the same height ($h$). The formula is: $v = \sqrt{2gh}$

Key Applications

  • Hydraulic Engineering: Designing spillways, drains, and discharge outlets for dams and reservoirs.
  • Fire Safety: Calculating flow rates from fire hydrants and hoses.
  • Industrial Pipelines: Estimating fluid discharge speeds in manufacturing and processing plants.
