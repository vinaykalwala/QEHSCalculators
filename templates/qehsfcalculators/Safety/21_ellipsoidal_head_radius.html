About 2:1 Ellipsoidal Heads

A 2:1 ellipsoidal head is a common type of end closure for pressure vessels. The "2:1" ratio refers to its major axis being twice the length of its minor axis. Calculating the effective spherical radius is a crucial step in designing the head to safely withstand internal pressure according to industry codes like ASME.

Accurate spherical radius calculations ensure proper stress distribution, structural stability, and material optimization in engineering applications.
