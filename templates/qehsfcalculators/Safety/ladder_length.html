üìò About This Calculator

The Ladder Length Calculator helps determine the ideal length of a ladder needed for a given vertical height. Proper ladder length is essential for ensuring safety and maintaining the recommended setup angle.

‚ùì How to Use This Calculator

  • Vertical Height (VH): Enter the height you need to reach, measured from the ground to the point of support (in meters).
  • The calculator applies the 4:1 rule, which means for every 4 meters of height, the ladder base should be 1 meter away from the wall.
  • It uses the Pythagorean theorem to calculate the actual ladder length and includes a safety margin of 1 meter.

  • Vertical Height: The height to be reached (in meters)
  • Base Distance: Calculated using the 4:1 ratio rule
  • Safety Margin: Additional 1 meter for safe access above the support point

üìä Safety Standards

This calculator follows OSHA and ANSI ladder safety standards:

  • 4:1 ratio for proper ladder angle (75.5 degrees)
  • 1-meter safety extension above the support point
  • Non-slip base requirements
  • Three-point contact climbing technique
