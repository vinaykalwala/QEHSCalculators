üìè Governing Principle

A Steam Valve Pressure Drop Calculator is based on standards for control valve sizing, with the primary international standard being IEC 60534 (equivalent to the European standard BS EN 60534). These standards, including the predecessor DIN EN 60534, define the flow coefficient (Cv or Kv) used to calculate pressure drop and valve sizing based on flow rate.

Key Standards and Concepts

  • IEC 60534 / BS EN 60534: Fundamental international and European standards providing methodology for sizing control valves.
