üìò About Safety Stock

**Safety Stock** is the extra inventory held by a company to prevent stockouts caused by unpredictable fluctuations in customer demand and supplier lead times. Calculating the optimal safety stock is a critical balancing act: too little risks lost sales and customer dissatisfaction, while too much ties up capital in unnecessary inventory.

‚öôÔ∏è Formula Used

The standard formula for safety stock is:

$$ \text{Safety Stock} = Z \times \sigma_d \times \sqrt{LT} $$

  • $Z$ = The Z-score corresponding to your desired service level.
  • $\sigma_d$ = The standard deviation of daily demand.
  • $LT$ = The lead time in days.
