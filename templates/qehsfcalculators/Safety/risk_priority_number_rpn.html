📘 About This Calculator

The Risk Priority Number (RPN) Calculator is used in Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) to quantify and prioritize risks. It combines Severity, Occurrence, and Detection ratings to highlight which risks need urgent attention.

⚙️ Parameters Used

  • Severity (1–10): How serious the effect of the potential failure is.
  • Occurrence (1–10): How frequently the failure is likely to occur.
  • Detection (1–10): How easy it is to detect the failure before it happens.

✅ Input Validation Rules

  • All inputs must be whole numbers (no decimals).
  • Acceptable range: 1–10 for Severity, Occurrence, and Detection.
  • No blank, zero, or negative values are allowed.
  • Severity = 10 → Critical risk regardless of other values.
  • Occurrence ≥ 8 and Detection = 10 → Very High risk flagged.
  • Valid RPN range: 1–1000.

📊 Output Validations

The calculator provides a Risk Assessment based on RPN value. The same feedback is shown in the results:

  • 1–50 → ✅ Low risk – Continue monitoring but no immediate action needed.
  • 51–150 → 🔵 Moderate risk – Consider preventive actions to reduce risk.
  • 151–250 → ⚠️ High risk – Immediate corrective actions should be taken.
  • 251–1000 → ❌ Critical risk – Urgent intervention is required to prevent failure.

📋 Industry Standards

These rules align with AIAG-VDA FMEA guidelines, ISO 14971 (Medical Devices), and other industry-specific quality and safety frameworks used in manufacturing, healthcare, aerospace, and automotive.
