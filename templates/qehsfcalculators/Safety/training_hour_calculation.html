This tool is designed for planners, trainers, and managers to easily quantify the time commitment of a training program. By inputting the schedule, you can quickly see the total and monthly time investment in both hours and equivalent 24-hour days, aiding in resource planning and scheduling.

‚öôÔ∏è Formula Used

The total training time in hours is calculated by finding the total minutes and converting.

$$ \text{Total Hours} = \frac{\text{Months} \times \text{Sessions} \times \text{Minutes per Session}}{60} $$

The equivalent days are then found by dividing the total hours by 24.
