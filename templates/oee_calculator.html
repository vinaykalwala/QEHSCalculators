Input & Output Standards, Validation, and Compliance

Input Validation: All user inputs are validated to ensure accurate OEE calculations.

  • Shift Start/End: Must be valid times in HH:MM format. Shift end can be past midnight.
  • Planned/Unplanned Downtime: Must be between 0 and 1440 minutes (24 hours).
  • Total Parts Produced: Must be a non-negative number, max 1,000,000.
  • Ideal Cycle Time: Must be >0 and ≤1440 minutes.
  • Scrap: Must be between 0 and total parts produced.

Output Validation & Interpretation: The calculator provides KPIs (Key Performance Indicator) with color-coded interpretation for quick insight:

  • OEE ≥ 90%: Excellent! Your production is running efficiently. Keep maintaining your high standards.
  • OEE 80–89%: Good performance, but there is room for improvement in downtime or quality.
  • OEE 50–79%: Warning! Consider addressing bottlenecks and scrap reduction to improve OEE.
  • OEE < 50%: Critical! Immediate action required to optimize operations and reduce losses.
These values are applied to Availability, Performance, Quality, and overall OEE. Feedback is generated to guide actions.

Standards & Compliance:

  • OEE calculation follows the international OEE standard (Availability × Performance × Quality).
  • Color codes and thresholds comply with common industry practices for manufacturing efficiency monitoring.
  • Ensures that all calculations are mathematically validated before output display.
